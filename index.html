7

I was a bit intrigued when I heard about how Ares Expedition was going to be a Terraforming Mars-lite - even moreso when I heard it was going to incorporate an action selection mechanic along the lines of Race for the Galaxy. I did not like Terraforming Mars - I felt it was fiddly, loose, and far too long for what it is - however I love Race for the Galaxy which is probably the single game I've played the most of. Unfortunately I've since gotten rid of Race because I've found it too difficult to teach in person, and I've been craving an RftG-lite ever since...

The Witcher: Old world

Eve Geek

10

In this game I liked the fact that you don't have to wait so long and build your character before dealing your first hit, fighting the monster for the first time. A few rounds and you can already try to face the first monsters, I like it very much since the game has a tension and extiement from the very first turn. Remember about the balance between exploring the world and fighting. If you ignore the fight, you probably won't win. On the other hand, if you ignore the story aspect completely, the storyline of the game, then you probably won't be able to develop your character enough to win fights with monsters. So you probably won't win as well.
